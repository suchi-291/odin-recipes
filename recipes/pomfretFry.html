Coastal Andhra's most loved Pomfret Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized pomfret fish, cleaned and slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tablespoons rice flour
  • 2-3 tablespoons semolina (rava)
  • Oil for frying
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

1. Prepare the Marinade:

  1. In a bowl, mix ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, lemon juice, and salt to form a smooth paste.

2. Marinate the Fish:

  1. Apply the marinade paste all over the cleaned pomfret fish, ensuring it gets into the slits.
  2. Cover and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, preferably 1 hour, in the refrigerator.

3. Coat the Fish:

  1. In a plate, mix rice flour and semolina.
  2. Coat the marinated fish evenly with the rice flour and semolina mixture.

4. Fry the Fish:

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, gently place the coated fish in the pan.
  3. Fry the fish for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until it turns golden brown and crispy.
  4. Remove the fish from the pan and place it on paper towels to drain excess oil.

5. Serve:

  1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with lemon wedges.
  2. Enjoy your delicious Pomfret Fry!
